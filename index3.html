A sage paragraph, in the context of academic writing, refers to a paragraph that uses wisdom literature and discourse from philosophy, history, politics, and economics to instruct and guide the reader on contemporary issues, often in a non-technical manner. It's a form of writing that goes beyond simple narration or description, aiming to offer insights and guidance.
